Projects & events

UPAA Germany’s projects and endeavors can be broadly categorized as:

  • The UPAA Germany Scholarship Grant
  • Research and Academic Cooperations
  • Cultural Exchange (through organizing workshops, cultural presentations, exhibits)
  • Education and Information Dissemination (e.g. educational tours, seminars, lectures, production of orientation booklet, book donation)
  • Outreach Projects and Social Events

Members and non-members who wish to help and get-involved in any of the current projects, please reach out. We also welcome additional projects that you wish to suggest and implement in collaboration with UPAA Germany.
