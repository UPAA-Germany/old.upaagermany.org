2017 October 10 - 24
Carlos Esguerra's photography exhibition of "Philippine Contemplative Landscape"
Mr. Carlos Esguerra is an internationally awarded Filipino fine art photographer based in New York.  Mr. Esguerra studied Business and Economics at the University of the Philippines and obtained his MBA from the George Washington University in Washington, DC. Since 1999, he has received numerous awards and recognition in photography. He retired early from his business in 2003 to devote his time to photography. For more info, please visit, www.carlosesguerra.com. 

Date:  October 10 - 24, 2017; Mondays to Fridays, 0900-1100h and 1300h-1600h.  Public viewing is possible by appointment through culture@philippine-embassy.de
Venue: Embassy of the Philippines, OG, Haus Cumberland, Kurfürstendamm 194, 10707 Berlin, Germany)

Note:  After the exhibit, the artworks will be sold by Mr. Esguerra to interested individuals.  A portion of the sale of the artworks will be donated to UPAA Germany's scholarship fund.

2017 September 11 - 22 Lakbay: An Exhibit
(a Talento project in cooperation with the Embassy of the Philippines and the UPAA Germany e.V.)
Lakbay (a Filipino word for Journey) is featuring 10 Berlin-based Filipino artists of different genres and shall emphasize on the plight of Filipinos while working and living abroad. During the Vernissage, there will be a talk not only about the artists’ showcased works but will also touch on migration issues affecting women.

Date:  September 11 - 22, 2017 (0900H - 1100H;  1300H -  1600H) - Public viewing by appointment through culture@philippine-embassy.de
Venue: Embassy of the Philippines, OG, Haus Cumberland, Kurfürstendamm 194, 10707 Berlin, Germany)

The exhibit's Vernissage is on September 8, 2017, 1600H - 1800H. 
Venue: Embassy of the Philippines, OG, Haus Cumberland, Kurfürstendamm 194, 10707 Berlin, Germany)
Vernissage RSVP by September 4, 2017 at culture@philippine-embassy.de
