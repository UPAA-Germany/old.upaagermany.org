HISTORICAL TOur: Memorial and Museum Sachsenhausen

Source: Lozano-Kühne, J. 2014. Sachsenhausen Photos. Private Collection.
This is a historical tour of the Concentration Camp Sachsenhausen.  It was built by prisoners after 'Reichsführer SS' Heinrich Himmler took charge of the German police in July 1936. More than 200,000 people were imprisoned in Sachsenhausen.  The prisoners include political opponents of the national socialist regime, people declared by the national socialists to be racially or biologically inferior and citizens from occupied European countries.  For more info, click here.
